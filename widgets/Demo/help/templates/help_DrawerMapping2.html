Click a National or Community name in the list and the map will zoom to that geographic extent.

To create your own spatial bookmark, position the map to the extent you would like to bookmark and enter the new bookmark name in the text box. Click the plus to bookmark the current view.

Your new spatial bookmark will appear in the Enhanced Bookmarks list and function as the other bookmarks do.

NOTE: Your new spatial bookmark is saved in your browser. Clearing your browser cache will delete your bookmark.

Create a Map title, then select a Layout and Format from the dropdown lists. For additional options, click Advanced.

Click Print. The graphic file becomes available in the list. Multiple maps may be added to the list.

Click a map title to open the map in a new browser tab. You may then save the graphic file locally or print it.

Add a point, line, polygon, or text to your map using the tools. Hover over the tools to determine the drawing mode.

Different options for styling the appearance of the drawing are available depending on the type of drawing element selected (point, line, or polygon). Once the styling has been selected, you may draw your shape interactively in the map.

Text elements may be customized with font style and placement options. Text entered into the Name box will be placed on the map at the location you select in the map, positioned according to your placement setting.

Click the Legend tool to display the map legend for all layers displayed.
