Elevation Profile

The Elevation Profile tool generates an elevation profile graph based on a transect line interactively drawn in the map. Elevation data are reported in units above mean sea level.

  • Click the ruler icon.
  • Select the units for the transect and elevation values.
  • Choose the map location to start the transect.
  • A single mouse click starts the transect.
  • Additional single mouse clicks add angles and bends along the transect.
  • A double mouse click ends the transect at your selected end point.
  • View the transect in the map.
  • View the elevation profile in the Profile Result tab.
