Enhanced Bookmarks

Use Enhanced Bookmarks to quickly zoom the interactive map directly to the conterminous USA or to an EnviroAtlas community, or create your own spatial bookmarks that can be saved and reused.

  • Click a National or Community name in the list and the map will zoom to that geographic extent.
  • To create a custom bookmark, position the map to the desired extent, enter the new bookmark name in the text box, click the plus sign.
  • Custom bookmarks are saved in the browser cache and will appear the next time you open the EnviroAtlas interactive map.
