EnviroAtlas Data

The EnviroAtlas Data tool offers hundreds of spatial data layers that may be added to the interactive map. The layers, mapped at a national or community extent, depict environmental metrics calculated or modeled by EPA and its partners.

  • Click a colored theme name to expand and view data layers associated with that theme.
  • Click the circular i button to the right of the data layer name to view the layer's description.
  • Check the box to the left of the data layer name to add the layer to the map.
