To view subbasins and subwatersheds within the stream network, perform the following:

  1. Select your desired Navigation direction, Upstream or Downstream.
  2. Click a point on the map. Subbasins and subwatersheds upstream or downstream of the selected location will be returned in the map. The Results tab in the tool pop-up shows statistics about the network of hydrologic units.

Upstream navigation

When you choose to view Upstream hydrologic units, the map displays as follows:

  • Hydrologic units nearest your selected location are displayed at the high-resolution subwatershed (HUC-12) level, displayed as pale green in the example above.
  • Hydrologic units further upstream are displayed at the lower-resolution subbasin (HUC-8) level, displayed as pink in the example above.
This is to speed processing and display of the map.

Downstream navigation

When you choose the Downstream option, the map displays high-resolution subwatersheds (HUC-12) down the stream network from your selected location to the outlet.

Clear results

To clear the results from the map, click Clear on the Select Subwatershed tab.

Export options

You may export the results of your HUC navigation. Multiple options are available; to access the export options, click the Results tab in the tool pop-up, then click the ellipsis.
