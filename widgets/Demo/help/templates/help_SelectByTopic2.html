The Select data by topic pop-up lists all of the EnviroAtlas Data theme topics as well as four geographic extents.

Select by theme

To limit the availability of data layers by theme, click on the theme name. Multiple themes may be selected and appear in bold text. This results in a reduced list of themes (and associated data layers) displayed in the left-side column.

Select by National or Community

To show only highly-detailed EnviroAtlas community data layers in the left-side column, click the EnviroAtlas Communities checkbox. Data themes that have no community layers show as greyed-out. The list of themes in the left-side column reduces accordingly. Likewise, to limit the selection to only national data layers, click the National checkbox to make only National extent layers available.

Select by geography

You may limit the availability of EnviroAtlas community data layers by geography. Click one of the Geography buttons at the bottom of the pop-up and the list of themes in the left-side column are reduced to only themes and layers that contain data layers for the selected geography.

Clear all topics

Click the clear all topics checkbox in the top bar to restore the full data theme list.
