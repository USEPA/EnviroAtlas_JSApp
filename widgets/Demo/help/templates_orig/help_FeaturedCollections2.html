Featured collections allows two ways to load prepared web maps into the EnviroAtlas interactive map:

  • Public – Provides a selection of EnviroAtlas-curated web maps illustrating how EnviroAtlas functionality may be used to answer environmental questions and make decisions.
  • My Content – Provides access to web maps you have assembled in your EPA GeoPlatform account.

Public

Choose a featured collection and select "Add to map" to load into the interactive map.

You may be given the option to "Cancel and save your current session" or "Proceed". Selecting "Proceed" opens the feature collection layers in the map and in the Layer List. You may now explore the map layers.

My Content

If you have an EPA GeoPlatform Online account, you may select "My Content" to add web maps from your GeoPlatform account.

After signing in, a list of your web maps will appear. Select a map and click "Add to map" to mash up your data layers with EnviroAtlas layers in the interactive map.
