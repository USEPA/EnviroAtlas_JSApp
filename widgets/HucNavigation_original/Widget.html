This tool is an inital release and is still under review and testing.


Click on any location in the conterminous US, navigate to the nearest stream, and then identify upstream or downstream 12-dgit HUCs within the stream network.


Under Settings, you may choose to select HUCs by distance measured in stream kilometers or by limiting the number of 12-digit HUCs. You may also export the result as a shape file by selecting this option before the search has started.


Click "Activate Tool", then "Select Point" to begin using this tool.
