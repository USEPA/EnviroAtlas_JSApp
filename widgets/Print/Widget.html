To print the map, EnviroAtlas suggests one of the following processes.

  • Close the left Table of Content menu ("<<" at the bottom of the panel)
  • Use one of these options in Windows:
    • Right-click your mouse to bring up the menu containing "print" tool.
    • Open a desktop tool such as "Snip & Sketch" or "Snipping Tool" to capture the map window.
    • Use the Alt-Print Screen keys and paste the capture into a picture or editing tool (Paint, Word, etc.)
    • Use the print function (Crtl-P) in your browser (i.e., Chrome or Edge) to capture the browser window.
  • For Mac OS, use one of these options:
    • Use Shift-Command-3 keys in combination for a screenshot "print" of your desktop or,
    • Use Shift-Command-4 keys in combination for capturing part of your screen.
