This tool gives you the ability to extract and map selected water quality data from the Water Quality Portal (WQP). The WQP (www.waterqualitydata.us) is a cooperative effort sponsored by the United States Geological Survey, the Environmental Protection Agency, and the National Water Quality Monitoring Council to provide publicly available water quality data in an integrated portal.
